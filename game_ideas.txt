Objective:
- Manage, upgrade and buy a new ship (early 19th century)
- manage your crew between your ship and prizes
Core Activities
- buy, trade and/or loot goods
- take prizes (and aristocratic prisoners) to sell for money
- fight for or against various nations
Core features
- large map (traverse still focused on your ship)
- learn where you are by spending time to observe the stars
- changing weather / wind patters
- real time combat (maneuver, range your guns, choose when to fire, boarding)
- training your crew (loading time, manoeuvre reaction time)
- manage supplies, ammo, gunpowder & food
- crew injuries & medical
- firefighting
